# Workflow Examples by Business Process

This reference provides comprehensive examples of workflow names and descriptions organized by business process within each domain.

## Sales Domain

### Business Process: Lead Generation
- **Prospect Research**: Identify and gather intelligence on potential clients using web search and LinkedIn. Produces contact database with decision-maker details.
- **Referral Tracking**: Monitor and document referral sources and conversion rates. Generates referral attribution report.
- **Content Lead Capture**: Track content downloads and webinar registrations. Creates qualified lead list from engagement data.

### Business Process: Lead Qualification
- **Initial Screening**: Assess prospect fit using qualification criteria. Produces qualified/disqualified determination.
- **Discovery Call Prep**: Research prospect background and prepare discussion points. Creates customized call agenda.
- **Needs Assessment**: Evaluate prospect requirements against service offerings. Generates fit analysis and recommendations.

### Business Process: Student Enrollment
- **Waitlist Conversion**: Send targeted email sequences to Maven waitlist. Produces enrollment completions and payment confirmations.
- **Payment Processing**: Handle student payments and generate confirmations. Delivers payment receipts and enrollment records.
- **Enrollment Confirmation**: Send welcome communications and next steps. Creates confirmation emails with access details.

### Business Process: Enrollment Recovery
- **Abandoned Cart Follow-up**: Re-engage prospects who didn't complete enrollment. Generates personalized recovery emails.
- **Waitlist Re-engagement**: Nurture cold waitlist prospects with valuable content. Produces monthly nurture campaign.
- **Objection Handling**: Address common enrollment barriers through targeted outreach. Creates customized objection response emails.

## Marketing Domain

### Business Process: Content Marketing
- **Blog Post Creation**: Write and publish educational content. Produces SEO-optimized articles.
- **Video Content Production**: Record and edit educational videos. Creates polished video assets.
- **Podcast Publishing**: Produce and distribute podcast episodes. Delivers published episodes with show notes.

### Business Process: Social Media Management
- **Post Scheduling**: Plan and schedule weekly social content. Creates content calendar with queued posts.
- **Engagement Monitoring**: Track comments and mentions across platforms. Generates engagement report with response recommendations.
- **Hashtag Research**: Identify trending and relevant hashtags. Produces optimized hashtag sets.

### Business Process: Email Marketing
- **Newsletter Composition**: Write and design weekly newsletter. Creates formatted newsletter ready to send.
- **List Segmentation**: Categorize subscribers by interests and behavior. Produces targeted subscriber segments.
- **Campaign Analytics**: Track open rates, clicks, and conversions. Generates campaign performance report.

## Product Domain

### Business Process: Course Development
- **Module Structuring**: Organize course content into logical modules. Creates course outline with learning flow.
- **Lesson Scripting**: Write detailed lesson scripts and talking points. Produces instructor guide with timing.
- **Resource Compilation**: Gather and organize course materials and references. Creates student resource library.

### Business Process: Content Creation
- **Slide Deck Design**: Create presentation slides for lessons. Produces visually consistent slide deck.
- **Exercise Development**: Design hands-on activities for students. Creates exercises with solutions and rubrics.
- **Case Study Writing**: Develop real-world examples for instruction. Produces detailed case studies with discussion questions.

### Business Process: Product Iteration
- **Feedback Analysis**: Review student feedback and identify improvements. Generates prioritized enhancement list.
- **Content Updates**: Revise course materials based on feedback. Creates updated lesson versions.
- **Testing & QA**: Verify course materials and technical functionality. Produces tested, production-ready content.

## Education Domain

### Business Process: Course Delivery
- **Session Facilitation**: Teach scheduled live classes. Delivers engaging learning experience.
- **Breakout Management**: Coordinate small group activities during sessions. Creates productive collaborative work time.
- **Recording Distribution**: Share session recordings with students. Produces accessible recording links.

### Business Process: Student Support
- **Office Hours Scheduling**: Coordinate one-on-one student meetings. Maintains organized meeting calendar.
- **Question Response**: Answer student questions via Slack and email. Generates helpful, timely responses.
- **Technical Troubleshooting**: Help students resolve platform access issues. Creates resolved technical problems.

### Business Process: Assessment & Feedback
- **Assignment Grading**: Review and score student submissions. Produces graded work with scores.
- **Feedback Writing**: Provide constructive improvement guidance. Creates detailed feedback comments.
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor student completion and engagement. Generates student progress reports.

## Consulting Domain

### Business Process: Engagement Management
- **Project Planning**: Define scope, timeline, and deliverables. Creates engagement plan document.
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Coordinate with client decision-makers. Produces alignment meeting notes.
- **Status Reporting**: Update clients on project progress. Generates weekly status reports.

### Business Process: Strategic Consulting
- **Discovery Session**: Conduct initial client assessment meetings. Creates needs analysis document.
- **Strategy Development**: Design AI implementation roadmap. Produces strategic recommendation deck.
- **Workshop Facilitation**: Lead client team through planning exercises. Delivers actionable workshop outcomes.

### Business Process: Implementation Support
- **Tool Configuration**: Set up AI tools for client use. Creates configured production environment.
- **Training Delivery**: Teach client teams to use new systems. Produces trained, capable team.
- **Change Management**: Guide organizational adoption process. Creates change communication plan.

## Operations Domain

### Business Process: Email Management
- **Inbox Triage**: Categorize and prioritize incoming emails. Produces organized action list.
- **Response Drafting**: Create replies to routine inquiries. Generates draft email responses.
- **Follow-up Tracking**: Monitor pending email responses. Creates follow-up reminder list.

### Business Process: Meeting Management
- **Agenda Preparation**: Create structured meeting agendas. Produces organized agenda document.
- **Note Taking**: Document key points and action items. Creates meeting minutes.
- **Action Item Tracking**: Monitor completion of meeting tasks. Generates action item status report.

### Business Process: System Administration
- **Tool Integration**: Connect and configure business systems. Creates integrated tool ecosystem.
- **Access Management**: Grant and revoke user permissions. Maintains secure access controls.
- **Data Backup**: Ensure critical information is preserved. Creates backup copies of important data.

## Finance Domain

### Business Process: Revenue Management
- **Invoice Generation**: Create and send client invoices. Produces professional invoices.
- **Payment Tracking**: Monitor received payments and outstanding balances. Creates accounts receivable report.
- **Revenue Recognition**: Record earned income in accounting system. Produces accurate financial records.

### Business Process: Expense Management
- **Receipt Processing**: Categorize and file business expenses. Creates organized expense records.
- **Reimbursement Handling**: Process team expense reimbursements. Generates reimbursement payments.
- **Budget Monitoring**: Track spending against budget allocations. Creates budget variance report.

### Business Process: Financial Planning
- **Forecast Development**: Project future revenue and expenses. Produces financial forecast model.
- **Metrics Tracking**: Monitor key financial KPIs. Generates executive dashboard.
- **Goal Progress**: Track progress toward financial targets. Creates goal attainment report.
